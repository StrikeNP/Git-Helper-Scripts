! The following group of figures make the Arakawa C grid used in WRF more
! graphically understandable.  The grid will be shown from seven different
! angles -- six two-dimensional cross-sectional views and a 3D look at a small
! cubic sector.
!
! U stands for locations where horizontal momentum variables in the x-direction
!    (east-west), which are the u wind component and it's tendencies, are
!    calculated.
! V stands for locations where horizontal momentum variables in the y-direction
!    (south-north), which are the v wind component and it's tendencies, are
!    calculated.
! W stands for locations where vertical momentum variables, which are the w wind
!    component and it's tendencies, are calculated.  Additionally, geopotential
!    (phb, ph) is calculated at W points.  Other variables that are calculated
!    here have 'w' in there names (examples of this are dnw, znw, dzw, z_at_w,
!    rdzw, etc.).
! THERMO stands for locations where thermodynamic and all other major variables
!    are calculated, such as pressure, theta, mixing ratios, etc.
!
! Brian Griffin (file created 11/24/2004; revised 6/11/2010)
!-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
!
!                         A Small 3-Dimensional Cube Sector

!                        [1 unit X by 1 unit Y by 1 unit Z]

!                     /--------------W--------------/  |
!                    /.  .          /.  .          /|  |
